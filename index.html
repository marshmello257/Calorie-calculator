What is CalCounter?

CalCounter is an AI-powered calorie tracker and counter that revolutionizes how you monitor your nutrition. Our innovative platform allows users to track calorie data by simply snapping a photo of their food.

Beyond basic tracking, CalCounter offers personalized recipes through an intelligent chat interface and provides various essential tools including a BMI calculator, deficit calculator, and a comprehensive food calorie database.

Whether you're looking to lose weight, maintain a healthy lifestyle, or simply understand your nutrition better, CalCounter makes it effortless and accurate.
